This project arises from the lack of real solutions big companies offer to solve the food waste problem.According to the EPA around 63.1 million tons of food waste was generated in the commercial, institutional, and residential sectors in 2018, this sum represents a 21.6% of total MSW generation. Great solutions have been developed by passionate individuals and businesses. However, we believe that we must contribute in some way and become part of the solution.

We also believe that the fact that those nearly expired products are not being donated ti charities and low-income families is a big deal. Great corporations rather destroy that food than give it to the people who are in need. This is why, we are willing to give 20% of our profits to charities that help with world hunger a poverty.

Working together for a better world.
